# Vision

## What PersonalAgentKit is for
PersonalAgentKit is for one person who wants a digital partner that can carry work forward without turning into a black box. It keeps a visible record of what it tried, what it learned, what it costs, and where it will pick up next, so the operator can trust it with meaningful goals over time.

## Who it's for
It is for technically capable solo operators such as independent builders, consultants, and small-business owners who juggle too many ongoing responsibilities to hold everything in their own head. They do not want a team platform or a flashy autonomous employee; they want a system that learns their priorities, stays local and inspectable, and becomes more useful through repeated use.

## What we will not be
We will not be a multi-tenant agent platform, an admin console for teams, or a cloud service that hides state behind hosted infrastructure. We will not chase breadth for its own sake through many channels, many agents, or autonomy theater if that weakens trust, clarity, or usability.

## Initiatives

### Durable By Default
What: Make memory, run history, decisions, and state human-readable local artifacts by default.
Why: Research and operator philosophy both point to trust growing when the system's behavior is inspectable rather than hidden.

### Resume Without Drama
What: Build clean restart and resumption behavior for long-running goals, with explicit progress state and recovery points.
Why: The biggest product gap surfaced by the research is reliable continuity across time, not more channels or more theatrics.

### Operator In The Loop
What: Keep approval boundaries, cost visibility, and communication conventions explicit at every layer of the system.
Why: PersonalAgentKit wins when it feels controlled and legible to one operator, not when it imitates unmanaged autonomy.

### Narrow, Polished Surfaces
What: Choose a small number of interactions that work exceptionally well for a single operator before expanding distribution.
Why: The strongest comparable products earn loyalty by matching a disciplined promise to their real operating model.

### Compound Capability
What: Turn successful work into reusable skills, stable schemas, and better future prompts instead of re-solving the same problem each cycle.
Why: The core promise is growth through experience, and that only becomes real when learning is captured in reusable form.

# Terroir

This file defines what this specific garden does and why it exists.
The coordinator writes this when creating a garden. The gardener reads
it during genesis alongside MOTIVATION.md, ROOT_SEED.md, and the charter.

A garden without a clear terroir will invent its own purpose, and it may
not be the one you intended.
